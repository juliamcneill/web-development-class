My name is Julia McNeill, and I’m graduating senior at New York University who is passionate about building cataloging and education-based social platforms. I’m skilled in creating dynamic web applications using a wide range of programming languages, frameworks, and tools, and I aim to use these skills to create user-centered and visually attractive platforms that integrate aspects of learning theory, cataloguing mechanics, and social networking.

I’m currently studying Art History, Computing and Data Science, and Web Development. Recently, I’ve worked on developing exhibitions for National Geographic, designing user interfaces at NYU Information Technology, and managing all photo and video content for NYU’s student-run newspaper, among other work experiences.

When I'm not writing code, you can usually find me looking for geocaches, drinking seltzer, doing crossword and logic puzzles, reading Wikipedia plot summaries before watching films, making playlists, and dreaming about the next time I can go camping.
